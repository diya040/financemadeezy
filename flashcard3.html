Loan

A loan is a sum of money or an item that one entity lets another entity borrow. The borrower repays their debt to the lender within a specified period and may pay interest on it.

Related Terms

  • Interest
  • Principal Amount
  • Collateral
  • Assets

Bank

It is a financial institution that is licensed to accept checking and savings deposits and make loans.

Related Terms

  • Plastic Money
  • Passbook
  • ATM (Automated Teller Machine)
  • Bank Account

Income Tax Return

A form which a person is supposed to submit to the Income Tax Department of India.

Related Terms

  • Form 16
  • Gross Total Income
  • Assessment Year(AY)
  • Tax Reduction at Source(TDS)
